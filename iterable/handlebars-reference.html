Handlebars Functions Reference

ðŸ”— Supported Date and Time Format Strings

  • Year:
    yyyy (e.g., `2025`)

  • Month:
    M or
    MM (e.g., `1` or `01` for January)

  • Day of Month:
    d or
    dd (e.g., `1` or `01`)

  • Hour (24-hour):
    H or
    HH (e.g., `00` for midnight)

  • Minute:
    m or
    mm (e.g., `0` or `00`)

  • Second:
    s or
    ss (e.g., `0` or `00`)

  • Time Zone Offset:
    Z (e.g., `-04:00` or `+07:00`)

  • Day of Week (number):
    u (e.g., `1` for Monday)

  • Day of Week (full name):
    EEEE (e.g., `Thursday`)

  • Day of Year:
    D (e.g., `206` for July 24)

  • Date only:
    yyyy-MM-dd (e.g., `2000-01-01`)

  • Date and Time (seconds):
    yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss (e.g., `2000-01-01 00:00:00`)

  • Date and Time (seconds with timezone):
    yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss Z (e.g., `2000-01-01 00:00:00 -04:00`)

  • ISO 8601 (milliseconds with timezone):
    yyyy-MM-dd'T'HH:mm:ss.SSSZ (e.g., `2000-01-01T00:00:00.000-04:00`)

  • Short format:
    short (e.g., `6/19/17`)

  • Medium format:
    medium (e.g., `Jun 19, 2017`)

  • Long format:
    long (e.g., `June 19, 2017`)

  • Full format:
    full (e.g., `Tuesday, June 19, 2017`)

  • Two-digit year for age:
    yy

ðŸ”— Supported Time Units for Date Math Expressions

  • y: Years
  • M: Months
  • w: Weeks
  • d: Days
  • h or H: Hours
  • m: Minutes
  • s: Seconds
