Supported Date and Time Format Strings

Format String Example
yyyy 2025
M or MM 1 or 01 for January
d or dd 1 or 01
H or HH 00 for midnight
m or mm 0 or 00
s or ss 0 or 00
Z -04:00 or +07:00
u 1 for Monday
EEEE Thursday
D 206 for July 24
yyyy-MM-dd 2000-01-01
yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss 2000-01-01 00:00:00
yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss Z 2000-01-01 00:00:00 -04:00
yyyy-MM-dd'T'HH:mm:ss.SSSZ 2000-01-01T00:00:00.000-04:00
short 6/19/17
medium Jun 19, 2017
long June 19, 2017
full Tuesday, June 19, 2017
yy 17

Supported Time Units for Date Math Expressions

Unit Description
y Years
M Months
w Weeks
d Days
h or H Hours
m Minutes
s Seconds

{{#eq (array)}}...{{/eq}} 📋

{{#eq array1 array2}}
  The arrays are equal
{{else}}
  The arrays are not equal
{{/eq}}

{{eq (array)}} 📋

{{#and (gt array1.size 2) (eq array1 array2)}}
  The arrays are equal and contain more than two items
{{else}}
  The arrays are not equal, or they don't contain more than two items.
{{/and}}}

  The objects are equal and b is 2
{{else}}
  The objects are not equal or b is not 2
{{/and}}
